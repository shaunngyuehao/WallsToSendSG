Purpose

Easily select which gym to climb at next according to your needs.

Filter based on:

  • Price (Multi Pass, Day Pass, Season Pass, Student)
  • Proximity
  • Size
  • Wall Types (Boulder, Top Rope, Lead)
  • Additional Facilities (Shower, Pool, Gym, Sauna)
  • User Ratings
  • Boards Available (Moon, Tension, Kilter)

Additionally, view information such as:

  • Time Slots
  • Booking Requirements
  • Price for Additional Rentals
  • Socials, Website
  • Good Food Around the Area

Motivation

Unlike many other countries, Singapore has a severe lack of natural rock surfaces to climb.

Thankfully, we have a plethora of indoor rock climbing gyms to choose from.

However, as an avid rock climber myself, I often find myself struggling to keep track of the prices and locations of gyms.

This is especially so with gyms popping up or closing down every now and then.

Moreover, the updated information of each gym can be tedious or even impossible to obtain...

Therefore, I created this website to allow our community to freely and easily access such information.
